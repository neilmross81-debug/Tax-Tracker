Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

Tax Tracker ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Data: We do not collect or store your financial data on our servers. All information regarding your salary, tax, and overtime is stored locally on your device or in your personal secure cloud (e.g., iCloud/Google Drive) if enabled.

AI Assistant: When you use the AI Assistant, snippets of your query are processed by Google Gemini API to provide responses. No personal identifying information is sold to third parties.

2. Use of Information

We use the information you provide to calculate your taxes and provide personal financial insights. We do not track you across other apps or websites.

3. Data Security

We implement professional security measures to protect your data. However, as the data is stored on your device, the primary security depends on your device's own security (passcode, biometric lock).

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@taxtracker.app.
